How to roast garlic

If you haven’t tasted roasted garlic yet you will be surprised how delicious it can be and how easy its preparation is. You just need the patience to leave it to soften enough. After less than an hour it will be soft like butter and your kitchen will be full of fantastic smell. You can spread roasted garlic on some toast or add to any meal like soup, hummus or baked meat.

prep time:
5 min

cook time:
45 min

ingredients:
garlic head(s)
olive oil

Step one

Prepare the whole heads of garlic. Remove the dirt or clay. Do not wash the heads with water and keep them dry.

Step two

Peel off the top layer of the skin – just enough that the cloves still remain intact in their casings and ensuring no garlic cloves separate from the head.

Step three

Cut off the top of the garlic head in order the tips of all cloves are exposed for baking. If any ends of maybe smaller cloves are not cut off enough, slices them properly.

Step four

Prepare two muffin pans of the same size. Place the heads of garlic into each cup of one of the pans with cut side up

Step five

Preheat the oven to 200 degrees, hot air oven to 180 degrees. Pour 1-2 table spoons of extra virgin olive oil over each garlic head. Use cooking brush to coat each piece of garlic until all heads are fully coated with oil.

Step six

Use the second muffin pan as a cover and place it reversely over the pan with prepared heads of garlic.

Step seven

Leave the heads of garlic covered in the oven for 40 minutes - roast until golden and soft.

Step eight

That's it! Enjoy your delicious meal!
